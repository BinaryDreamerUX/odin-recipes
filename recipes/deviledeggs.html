This deviled eggs recipe is one of our favorites for the holidays.

Well, anytime, really!

Deviled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 12 eggs
  • Half cup homemade mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons dijon mustard
  • 3 tablespoons dill relish, drained
  • Pepper to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • Paprika garnish

Instructions

  1. Hard boil the eggs.
    • In a large pot, cover the eggs with water.
    • Put the pot on medium high heat and bring to a rolling boil.
    • Once at a rolling boil, remove from the heat, cover, and let sit for 10 minutes.
    • Place the eggs in an ice bath after 10 minutes to stop the cooking process.
  2. Peel the cooled eggs and slice in half lengthwise.
  3. Gently remove the yolks and place them in a mixing bowl.
  4. Mash the yolks until desired consistency.
  5. Add the mayonnaise, dijon, relish, pepper, and salt and combine to a creamy texture.
  6. Spoon the mixture into the half egg whites.
  7. Sprinkle the tops with paprika to garnish.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
