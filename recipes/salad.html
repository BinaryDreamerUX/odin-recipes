This is a quick and easy salad recipe.

Even though it's thrown together, it makes a great and healthy appetizer!

Salad

Ingredients

  • 5 cups loosely packed mixed greens
  • Quarter cup crumbled bacon
  • Quarter of a red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, thinly sliced or chunked
  • Bag of croutons
  • 1 cup freshly shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • Favorite dressings

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, bacon, onion, cucumber, tomato, and croutons.
  2. Toss all together.
  3. Add dressing to taste and toss again.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
