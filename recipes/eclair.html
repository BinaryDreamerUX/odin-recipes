This is our special no-bake eclair, which makes it super simple to make.

Just because it's no bake, doesn't make it any less delicious!

Chocolate Eclair

Ingredients

  • 2 small boxes vanilla instant pudding
  • 2 and half cups milk
  • 1- 8 ounce container Cool Whip
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 box graham crackers
  • 1 can chocolate frosting

Instructions

  1. Combine pudding mix and milk.
  2. Beat until well mixed, then stir in powdered sugar.
  3. Stir in Cool Whip until everything is combined well.
  4. Place a layer of graham crackers in the bottom of a 9x13" dish.
  5. Spoon a generous layer of pudding mixture over graham crackers.
  6. Add a layer of graham crackers on top of the mixture.
  7. Repeat the layering process of spooning the pudding mixture and laying the graham crackers until all mixture is used.
  8. Be sure to the last and top layer have graham crackers and the pudding mixture is not on the very top.
  9. Melt the chocolate frosting in the microwave for 30 seconds and stir.
  10. Pour the melted chocolate frosting over the top layer of graham crackers and use a spatula to smooth it to the edges.
  11. Let set overnight or at least 4 hours in the refrigerator.
  12. Serve and enjoy!
